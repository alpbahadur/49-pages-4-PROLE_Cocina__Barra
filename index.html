Our Story

De la Finca a la Mesa

After Hurricane Maria, Chef José Esteban Rivero and his wife Sarah Santos returned to their beloved Puerto Rico with a mission: to support local agriculture and create a gathering place for their community.

Founded in April 2019, PROLE Cocina & Barra represents more than just a restaurant. It's a commitment to Puerto Rico's resilience, a celebration of local farmers, and a testament to the power of fresh, seasonal ingredients.

With 11 years of culinary training in Philadelphia and Sarah's expertise in marketing and events, they've created a space where internationally-inspired cuisine meets authentic Puerto Rican hospitality.

"Our menu changes every few days because we believe in serving only the freshest ingredients from our local partners. Every dish tells a story of our island's agricultural heritage."

Supporting Local Agriculture

Every ingredient on our menu has a story. We work directly with local farmers, fishermen, and artisanal producers across Puerto Rico to bring you the finest seasonal ingredients.

This direct relationship ensures fair compensation for our partners while guaranteeing the freshest possible ingredients for your table. It's more than farm-to-table—it's family.

"Our commitment to local agriculture isn't just good business—it's our responsibility to the island that raised us and the community that welcomed us home."

— Chef José Esteban Rivero & Sarah Santos, Founders
